About

A safe pair of hands for the work that keeps your business moving.

I'm Rosemary — the person behind The Ress Brand. I partner with founders, executives and growing teams who are brilliant at what they do but drowning in the day-to-day. My job is simple: bring order, take initiative, and make sure nothing slips through the cracks.

I'm known for being genuinely hardworking, dependable and easy to work with. I communicate clearly, meet deadlines, and treat your business like it's my own. Give me the messy parts — the overflowing inbox, the scattered calendar, the half-finished spreadsheet — and I'll hand them back tidy.
